INSFVSymmetryVelocityBC

This object implements a symmetry boundary condition for the velocity. It applies boundary forces such that the gradient of the velocity parallel to the boundary is zero in the boundary normal direction. A INSFVSymmetryVelocityBC should be applied for every velocity component on a symmetry boundary. Similarly an INSFVSymmetryPressureBC should be applied for the pressure on the symmetry boundary.
