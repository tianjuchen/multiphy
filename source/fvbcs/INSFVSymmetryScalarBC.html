INSFVSymmetryScalarBC

This object ensures that the scalar quantity flux perpendicular to a symmetry boundary is zero. This is generally already achieved by the velocity and mass symmetry boundary conditions, which set the fluid normal velocity to zero, but may required in their absence.

If solving the fluid flow equations simultaneously, in addition to the INSFVSymmetryScalarBC, an INSFVSymmetryVelocityBC should be applied for every velocity component and an INSFVSymmetryPressureBC should be applied on the pressure, on a symmetry boundary.
