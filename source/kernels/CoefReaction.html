CoefReaction / ADCoefReaction

Implements the residual term (p*u, test)

Reaction with a scalar prefactor (coef) where (v) is a coupled variable.

commentnote

There is no FV (finite volume) version of CoefReaction. If you wish to use FV, use FVCoupledForce.
